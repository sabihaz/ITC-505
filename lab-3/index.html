How to Deliver Fonts to a Browser

Fonts can be delivered to a browser through various methods, but one of the most common and user-friendly methods is using Google Fonts. By including a specific link in your HTML, you can easily load a font hosted by Google on your website. This method minimizes the need for local font files and ensures that the font is accessible across all devices.

To use a Google Font, simply select the desired font, copy the provided link tag, and paste it into the head section of your HTML document. After that, you can specify the font in your CSS using the font-family property.
